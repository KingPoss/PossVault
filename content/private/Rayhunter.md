## Rayhunter and direction finding
If they're going to monitor us, we should monitor them.
How? With [Rayhunter](https://github.com/EFForg/rayhunter). 
Rayhunter is a project from the electronic frontier foundation, that uses a cheap, highly accessible mobile hotspot (orbic) to detect when a stingray is being used in your proximity.
It can give specifics about what attack vector is being used, but what we're interested in is using this in conjunction with tuned antennas for 3/4/5G and a portapack, giving us direction finding capabilities. Combining these technologies, you should be able to direction find the Stingray back to it's source. Most likely an unmarked van, or police car. Document the plates, the occupants inside, etc. etc.
