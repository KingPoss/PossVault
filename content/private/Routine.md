## Before work
1. Wake up before 9 AM, 8:30 AM preferably
2. Start coffee
3. Brush teeth
4. Take meds
5. Drink Chia
## After work
- Decompress in low sensory environment until recharged
- Exercise 15 minutes, 30 minutes preferably
- If craving it, engage in interests (Researching, cataloging, coding, art, etc. etc.)
## Before bed
- Take night time medication
- Brush teeth
- Make bed

## During weekend
1. Wake up before 11 AM
2. Start coffee or get energy/food
3. Brush teeth
4. Take meds
5. Drink Chia
6. Exercise, 30 minutes
