You need to leave the party

You need to leave the party. This place isn't for you. 
As you try to leave the party, you keep running into people who stop your progression.
They ask you questions, throw you into dialog that you need to find an out for. Perhaps if you choose the wrong out, something else happens (they agree to help or something) or maybe they send you back to do something for them before you can progress
You can try to sneak by certain people, others are impossible to sneak by
