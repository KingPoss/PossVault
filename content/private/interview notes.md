- Can you tell us about yourself?

- What’s the backstory of how you became a composer and artist? Was there a moment in your life that set you on this path as a creative?

- Who are some of your musical or artistic influences? Are there composers, visual artists, or anyone else who's been a source of inspiration for you?

- As you progressed in your career, were there any mentors or pivotal experiences that significantly shaped your approach, or confidence as an artist?

- What are some of the tools or software you use for music and multimedia creation? And how how has working with programs like Max/MSP or other tech tools influenced the kind of music you make?

- Being a multimedia artist, I feel comes along with being a sort of jack of all trades. does excelling in one area ever come at the expense of another, or do they all feed into each other?

- Has your artistic journey taught you any surprising lessons about yourself—your identity, your strengths, or even your weaknesses?

- Beyond the music and visuals themselves, what do you hope people take away from your art on a personal or emotional level?

- What role do you think digital spaces play in the arts today, especially for interdisciplinary work like yours? Have you explored things like online performances or virtual installations, and how do they compare to any in person experiences you've had?

- What advice would you give to young artists and musicians? Any lessons from your journey that you’d pass on to the next generation of creatives?
