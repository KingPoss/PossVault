# Goal
Create a voip-based telecoms "company" that offers services to users such as US phone numbers/line activation (no SMS, voice only), a party line for users to socialize together (audio conferences), and a way to leave voicemails that will appear for playback on the site, or through a dialing tree/IVR over the phone, similar to playatel. Foster human to human connection between webmasters and artists on the smallweb
# Why? 
Modern times have trained most of us to be anxious of phone calls. Just getting one from a friend rather than a text might have you sitting there going "Oh god, what happened?", dreading the worst. Maybe they make you nervous because you're thinking "what do I say?" or "what if they don't want to talk right now?" 
And what do you think when you get a phone call from a number you don't know? Probably "it's a robocall or spam", and I think it's a real pity that phones have completely lost their whimsy. Telephones made the world smaller and brought people closer together, and I feel that a lot of new technology has been doing the opposite, pulling us all away from each other. We're as interconnected as ever, but somehow, a lot of us feel more lonely than we ever did before. 
I propose we try and reconnect with each other, on a real human to human level. How cool would it be if you could receive a call from a fellow netizen that liked your website, and wanted to let you know directly? Or calling into a weekly party line with other people, and just shooting the breeze? 
Let's bring back the phone lines! 

# Rough plan outline
1. Create a webring/group, and gather users who are willing to use the system. Possibly incentivize participation by offering a month of free service and free line activation.
2. Create guides on how to set this up on as many devices as possible (Soft clients, ATAs, etc)
3. Host bi-weekly (or some other repeating interval) "party lines" (audio conferences) that users would be able to dial into. discuss different topics, possibly play games, etc.
4. A Yellow pages type directory for websites to host their phone numbers (if that's something people want)
5. A way for people to leave messages that can be played on the website, or over the phone ( like Playatel BBS https://bbs.playa.tel/ ) 
6. A way to report any harassing phone calls (likely report to me, I block the number in question and report call to FCC / local authorities)
