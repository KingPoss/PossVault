## Regarding Movie Screening
1. Is this during the day, or at night? Is it inside, or outside?
2. How big is the screen, what distance can we set the projector at?
3. Is this going to be with admission, or for free? 
4. How much for a projector?
5. Is there a sound system we plan on using, or do we need one of those too?
