# Ingredients 
- Mrs. Dash table blend
- Onion powder
- Garlic powder
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Cornish game hen
- Oil of some kind (olive, vegetable, etc.)
# Instructions
- Thaw hen
- Preheat air fryer to 350 F
- Oil it up
- Season it
- Cook for 20 minutes, flip, cook for another 20 minutes
- Eat the deliciousness you have just prepared
